About

I have found my Purpose - to help and serve others to overcome their bullying struggles.

The mission for the podcast is for listeners, especially young adults and adolescents, to reflect and draw inspiration from high profile guests, sharing their experiences and wisdom on the topics of bullying, self-awareness and their inner spirit.

Especially with bullying, it is a very private and personal matter.

Through the stories (and vulnerabilities) guests will share, you will come away with a unique understanding how their earlier adversity dealing with bullying helped:

  • Strengthen their Resolve
  • Shaped and Developed their Character
  • Taught Perseverance

…Ultimately leading them to become the successful person they are today.

Drawing inspiration from my daughter Riyani who was bullied in middle school because of her Tourette’s, she was severely teased and made fun of because of her uncontrollable motor and vocal tics.

As a maturing young adult, she's been a successful teen author, worked at a Ritz Carlton property for a year and is now attending Northern Arizona University.

The adversity she experienced growing up has led her to be person she is today.

It is my sincere hope that the listeners, including adults come away with nuggets of wisdom that they can internalize, taking a brief pause reflecting on the messaging from our guests, serving as good reminders of our inner spirit we all have.

Thank you for your interest in this podcast and God Bless.
